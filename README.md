# AI-Powered Mind Mapping Tools

Welcome to the **AI-Powered Mind Mapping Tools** repository! This list showcases some of the best tools that leverage artificial intelligence to assist with brainstorming, organizing ideas, and visualizing complex concepts. Whether you're working solo or in a team, these tools can boost creativity, collaboration, and productivity.

## Key Features to Consider
When selecting an AI-powered mind mapping tool, consider the following essential features:

- **User-Friendly Interface:** Intuitive design with minimal learning curve.
- **AI Assistance:** Intelligent suggestions for keywords, idea generation, and connections.
- **Collaboration:** Real-time collaboration features for team projects.
- **Third-Party Integrations:** Integration with tools like Google Workspace, Slack, etc.
- **Pricing:** Free plans or affordable paid options for different needs.

## List of AI-Powered Mind Mapping Tools

### [MindMap AI](https://mindmapai.app)
MindMap AI uses artificial intelligence to generate mind maps from text, PDFs, images, and audio, with an AI Copilot for idea generation.  
- **Key Features:** AI chatbot for brainstorming, multi-format support, and smart editing.
- **Pricing:** Free plan available.

### [XMind AI](https://www.xmind.net)
XMind AI offers idea expansion and mind map generation with support for multiple structures like tree charts and logic diagrams.  
- **Key Features:** AI brainstorming, multiple structures, task tracking.
- **Pricing:** Free trial, paid plans start at $9.99/month.

### [Boardmix](https://www.boardmix.com)
Boardmix provides an AI-powered whiteboard for mind mapping and team collaboration, offering insights and visualization features.  
- **Key Features:** AI-generated insights, drag-and-drop interface, collaboration tools.
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, paid plans start at $4.90/month.

### [Ayoa](https://www.ayoa.com)
Ayoa integrates AI for mind maps and task management, allowing for easy expansion of ideas during brainstorming sessions.  
- **Key Features:** AI idea generation, multiple view modes (Gantt charts, whiteboards).
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, paid plans start at $17/month.

### [Whimsical AI](https://whimsical.com)
Whimsical AI offers mind mapping, flowcharts, and wireframes, with AI suggestions to generate and connect ideas.  
- **Key Features:** AI-driven suggestions, integrations with Jira, Slack, and more.
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, paid plans start at $10/month.

### [Wondershare EdrawMind](https://www.edrawsoft.com)
EdrawMind provides AI-powered features for idea generation and map creation, along with a variety of templates for planning.  
- **Key Features:** AI-driven ideas, templates for brainstorming, strategy planning.
- **Pricing:** Subscription plan at $59/year, lifetime plan at $118.

### [GitMind](https://gitmind.com)
GitMind is a free mind-mapping tool with AI features to help generate summaries, main ideas, and branches.  
- **Key Features:** AI-powered summaries, customizable themes, unlimited nodes.
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, paid plans start at $2.19/month.

### [Lucidchart](https://www.lucidchart.com)
Lucidchart supports AI-enhanced mind mapping and diagram creation, with integrations for visual collaboration.  
- **Key Features:** AI idea generation, real-time collaboration, third-party integrations.
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, paid plans start at $7.95/month.

### [Jeda.ai](https://jeda.ai)
Jeda.ai generates mind maps, flowcharts, and diagrams using simple prompts and supports real-time collaboration.  
- **Key Features:** AI-driven diagram creation, infinite canvas, team collaboration.
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, paid plans start at $10/month.

### [Taskade](https://taskade.com)
Taskade combines task management with AI-powered mind maps, making it ideal for teams that want to brainstorm and plan simultaneously.  
- **Key Features:** AI-generated task lists and maps, Kanban boards, Slack/GitHub integrations.
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, paid plans start at $4/month.

### [MindMeister](https://www.mindmeister.com)
MindMeister supports real-time collaboration and AI-driven brainstorming, ideal for team-based projects.  
- **Key Features:** Collaboration, AI suggestions, task management integration.
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, paid plans start at $4.99/month.

### [Mindomo](https://www.mindomo.com)
Mindomo features AI-powered brainstorming and interactive presentations for real-time collaboration.  
- **Key Features:** AI-assisted idea generation, interactive presentation creation.
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, paid plans start at $6/month.

### [Miro](https://miro.com)
Miro offers AI-powered mind mapping, with features designed for team collaboration and integration with project management tools.  
- **Key Features:** AI-generated maps, infinite canvas, Trello/Jira integrations.
- **Pricing:** Free plan available, paid plans start at $10/month.
